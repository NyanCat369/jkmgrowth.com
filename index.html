FAQ

Common questions.

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How quickly can you start generating leads?
+

We can have your first batch of leads contacted within 24 hours of onboarding. All we need is your target business type and city.

What does the outreach look like to leads?
+

Each email is personalized to the specific business — referencing their location, business type, and a relevant pain point. It looks like a human wrote it, because our AI is trained to sound exactly that way.

What happens when a lead replies?
+

Replies go directly to your email inbox. You take over the conversation from there and close the deal. We handle all the outreach up to that point.

Do I need to sign a long-term contract?
+

No. The pay-per-meeting model has no contract — you pay only when we book a qualified call. The retainer is month-to-month with no lock-in.

What industries do you work with?
+

Any high-ticket B2B or B2C business where one client is worth $5,000+. We currently specialize in roofing, dental, med spas, and law firms but can work with most industries.
